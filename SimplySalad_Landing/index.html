Who are we?

Hello there! We're a cloud kitchen based in Ahmedabad. Our mission is to provide healthy, delicious salads with fresh, high-quality ingredients. One thing that sets us apart from other restaurants is our subscription-based model. This means that customers can sign up to receive weekly or monthly deliveries of our salads, making it easy to maintain a healthy diet without sacrificing taste or convenience. However, even if you're not a subscriber, you can still enjoy our salads through Swiggy and Zomato. So if you're looking for a new go-to spot for fresh, flavorful salads, look no further than The Simply Salad!
